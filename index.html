About me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Sanath Keshav. I am an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree (EMJMD) scholar, currently a graduate student of Computational Mechanics of materials and structures at the Institute for Applied Mechanics, Universität Stuttgart. Previously, I was a graduate student of Computational Mechanics at the International center for Numerical Methods in Engineering (CIMNE), Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Barcelona. I completed my Bachelor of Engineering (Honors) Mechanical Engineering from BITS Pilani - Goa campus, India.

I grew up in historic and sprawling city of Mysore, India. Coming from a family of academics, I inherited a fundamental urge to learn and achieve. I was always interested in designing and creating things from a young age. Having always been facinated by modern machines, I decided to pursue an undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering. I had the pleasure of undertaking my Bachelor thesis at the Barcelona supercomputing center (BSC), which was my first formal introduction to Computational science and High Performance computing. My brief experience in this field exhibited immense potential which led me to take on a career in Computational Mechanics and HPC. However, I don't restrict myself to these and practically love working on anything hands-on, on stuff that can benefit society. When I am not working, I prefer travelling, reading, playing lawn tennis, running and listening to music. I am an Indian at heart yet global in spirit.
